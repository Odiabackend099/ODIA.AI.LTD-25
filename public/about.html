About ODIADEV

We're headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria, with global operations. Our mission is to democratize voice AI for Africa, and our strategy is to integrate across telco, fintech, health, and public safety ecosystems.

Our Mission

ODIADEV exists to ensure that Africa's voice-first economy has an AI backbone built for its realities: accents, bandwidth constraints, device diversity, and the everyday need for reliable communication.

What Drives Us

  • Democratize Voice AI: Make advanced voice technology accessible to every African business
  • Accent-Aware Technology: Build systems that understand and respond to African accents naturally
  • Infrastructure Resilience: Create solutions that work on 3G networks and offline
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Respect and incorporate African communication patterns

Our Vision

A future where every African can interact with AI in their native language and dialect, where voice technology bridges the digital divide and creates opportunities for all.

Strategic Focus

  • Voice-First Economy: Empower Africa's primary communication interface
  • Telecom Integration: Work with carriers to reach every mobile device
  • Industry Partnerships: Collaborate across fintech, health, and safety sectors
  • Global Expansion: Scale our solutions across African markets

Our Story

Born in Lagos, built for Africa, delivered globally. Our journey reflects the reality that most AI systems are optimized for Western contexts‚Äînot African realities.

The Problem We Saw

Across Africa, voice remains the primary interface for communication, business, and daily life. Yet most AI voice systems struggled with African accents, required high bandwidth, and failed during network interruptions. This created a digital divide that excluded millions from voice AI benefits.

We realized that building voice AI for Africa meant understanding not just the technology, but the realities of African infrastructure, communication patterns, and user behaviors.

Our Solution

ODIADEV closes the gap with an integrated voice-first stack: fast inference on Groq LPU, scalable GPU on RunPod, reliable backend on Supabase, global delivery on Vercel, and a custom ODIADEV-TTS 1.6B model optimized for Nigerian English and Pidgin.

The result is real-time voice interactions that are inclusive, dependable, and easy to deploy across diverse African contexts‚Äîfrom Lagos to rural communities.

Our Impact

We're enabling businesses to serve more customers through voice AI, helping healthcare support reach more people, and ensuring communities access help faster‚Äîall while keeping costs predictable and deployments simple.

Every conversation that happens in Nigerian English or Pidgin, every call that gets answered during a network outage, every person who gets help through our emergency response systems‚Äîthat's our impact.

Our Future

We're building toward a comprehensive voice AI ecosystem across Africa. This includes expanding language support, improving offline capabilities, and partnering with local organizations to ensure our technology serves African needs first.

Our goal is to become the default voice AI infrastructure provider for Africa, powering the next generation of voice-first applications and services.
