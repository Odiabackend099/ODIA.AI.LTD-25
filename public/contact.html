Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about getting started with ODIADEV.

How quickly can we get started?

Most clients can be up and running within 1-2 weeks. Our sandbox environment is available immediately for testing, and our team can help with integration and deployment. Enterprise implementations with custom requirements may take 4-8 weeks.

What languages do you support?

We currently support Nigerian English and Nigerian Pidgin with high accuracy. We're actively working on expanding to other African languages including Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. Contact us for information about specific language support.

Do you offer trial or proof of concept?

Yes! We offer free sandbox access for development and testing. For production trials, we provide pilot programs with limited usage to demonstrate value before full deployment. Contact our sales team to discuss the best approach for your use case.

What about data privacy and compliance?

We're committed to data privacy and comply with GDPR, NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation), and other relevant frameworks. We offer data residency options, encryption at rest and in transit, and privacy-first configurations. Our SerenityCare AI defaults to no PHI storage.

How do you handle network connectivity issues?

Our solutions are built for African infrastructure realities. We offer 3G optimization, offline processing modes, and graceful degradation during network interruptions. This ensures continuous service even with inconsistent connectivity.
